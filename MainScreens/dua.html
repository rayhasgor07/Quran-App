EXTRA
DUA WHEN ANGRY أَعُوْذُ بِللّٰهِ مِنَ لشَّيْطَنِ رَّجِيْمِ I seek refuge in Allah from the accursed Shaitaan
DUA BEFORE DOING ANYTHING بِسْمِ ٱللَّٰهِ ٱلرَّحْمَٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ In the name of Allah, Most Gracious, Most Merciful
DUA BEFORE LEAVING HOME بِسْمِ اللَّهِ تَوَكَّلْـتُ عَلَى اللَّهِ، وَلاَ حَوْلَ وَلاَ قُـوَّةَ إِلاَّ بِاللَّهِ In the name of Allah, I place my trust in Allah. There is neither might nor power except with Allah.
DUA WHEN ENTERING THE MASJID اللَّهُمَّ افْتَحْ لِيْ أَبْوَابَ رَحْمَتِكَ O Allah! Open for me the doors of your mercy.
DUA WHEN LEAVING THE MASJID اَللَّهُـمَّ إِنِّي أَسْأَلُكَ مِنْ فَضْـلِكَ O Allah! Verily, I seek from You Your bounty.
DUA WHEN TRAVELLING سُبْحَانَ الذي سَخَّرَ لَنَا هذا، وَما كُنَّا له مُقْرِنِينَ، وإنَّا إلى رَبِّنَا لَمُنْقَلِبُونَ In the name of Allah and all praise is for Allah. How perfect He is, the One Who has placed this (transport) at our service and we ourselves would not have been capable of that, and to our Lord is our final destiny.
DUA FOR INCREASING KNOWLEDGE رَّبِّ زِدْنِيْ عِلْمًا My Lord! Increase my knowledge.
DUA AFTER SNEEZING الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ All praise is due to Allah.
DUA WHEN HEARING SOMEONE SNEEZE يَرْحَمُكَ اللَّهُ May Allah have mercy on you.
DUA WHEN SOMEONE DIES إِنَّا لِلَّهِ وَإِنَّآ إِلَيْهِ رَٰجِعُونَ Indeed we belong to Allah, and indeed to Him we will return.
